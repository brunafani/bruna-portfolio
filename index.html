Writing Samples: Masculinities & Digital Gender Politics

Produced in the context of applied research on digital masculinities and the manosphere among adolescent boys in Latin America, commissioned by Equimundo and Plan International (2025–2026). Both produced in Spanish, reflecting Bruna's professional multilingual practice.

Writing Sample A
Cross-Sectional Analysis Sheet — Brazil
Studying the Manosphere in Latin America | Equimundo & Plan International | Brazil, 2026
Comparative articulation of materials produced in two focus groups conducted in Brazil — with 13 and 15-year-old participants from Salvador (BA) and Vimao (RS). Analyses how the manosphere functions not primarily as ideology, but as affective infrastructure occupying the space left by absent adult guidance.
"No tienen personas cerca. Buscan una guia." — Gustavo, FG1, 13 years old, Salvador (BA)
Key findings: void of adult reference; coexistence of critical distance and real exposure; direct impact of sigma content on behaviour toward a female peer; spontaneous critical capacity among adolescents as an entry point for intervention design.
